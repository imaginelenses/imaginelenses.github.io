Hello!

It's me.
No, I'm just kidding.


I've been clicking pictures from a pretty young age. It's only in the recent years that I began doing it artistically. For the all these years I shot on Point and Shoots and on Phones. Only a year ago did I get my first Interchangeable Lens Camera.
I don't have the "Textbook" knowledge of Photography - I just experiment with the camera and understand it's capabilities. I take full advantage of the digital format by clicking as many pictures as required until I get the shot in my head.

On a more personal note, I love travelling and exploring new places and cultures. I love listening stories and I try to tell one in every image I create. I want to meet as many people as possible in life, listen to their stories and get to know as many of them as possible - I believe there is something to learn from each one of them.
I also love going on adventures like hiking, trekking, rock climbing, river rafting and zip-lining; skydiving and much more is on my long bucket list.
