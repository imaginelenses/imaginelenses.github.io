## {% title "About Me" %}

Hi, I'm **Karthik Shivashankaran**, a full-stack robotics engineer, computer vision engineer, and the person behind Imaginelenses.

I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Computer Engineering at New York University (NYU Tandon, Class of 2026), and hold a bachelor's degree in Mechatronics Engineering. As a 2026 Computer Engineering Graduate, I build at the intersection of hardware and software. My expertise spans sensor fusion, state estimation, motion/path planning, real-time embedded systems, and computer vision pipelines. I have experience writing production-deployed firmware (C/C++) and maintaining 130-star open-source Python tools.

Outside of engineering, I'm a <a href="/photo">photographer</a> who tries to tell a story in every frame, an avid traveler, and someone who genuinely loves meeting people and hearing what makes their life tick. Adventures are a constant — hiking, rock climbing, river rafting, and a long bucket list that keeps growing.

I'm actively looking for full-time roles as a robotics engineer, computer vision engineer, or motion/path planning engineer. If you're building something interesting, let's talk.
